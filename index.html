Questions? We've got answers.

Is my data secure?
Yes. We use Firebase with enterprise-grade security. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties.
Can my whole band use this?
Absolutely. Invite your bandmates and set their role. Admins can see and edit everything including finances. Members can view the schedule without seeing sensitive financial details.
Does it work offline?
The app works offline for viewing your data. Changes sync automatically when you're back online — perfect for venues with spotty wifi.
Can I manage multiple bands?
Yes! The free plan includes 1 band. Pro users can create and manage unlimited bands, perfect for musicians in multiple projects or band managers.
What if I need help?
We're musicians too — we get it. Reach out anytime at support@boply.io. Pro users get priority support with faster response times.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime with no questions asked. Your data remains accessible even after canceling — we don't hold it hostage.
