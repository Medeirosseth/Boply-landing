Questions? We've got answers.

When does Boply launch?
We're launching in February 2026 on iOS, Android, and Web. Join the waitlist to get early access and founding member pricing that's locked in forever.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use Firebase with enterprise-grade security. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties. You can export or delete your data anytime.
Can my whole band use this?
Absolutely. Invite your bandmates with a simple code or email. Admins can see and edit everything including finances. Members can view the schedule, vote on polls, and sell merch — without seeing sensitive financial details.
How does Sales Mode work?
Sales Mode turns your phone into a merch sales station. Select items, tap sizes, and complete sales in seconds. Inventory updates automatically and sales are logged to the current show. Works offline too — syncs when you're back online.
Does it work offline?
Yes! The app works offline for viewing data and making sales. Changes sync automatically when you're back online — perfect for venues with spotty wifi.
Can I manage multiple bands?
Yes! The free plan includes 1 band. Pro users can create and manage unlimited bands with one account — perfect for musicians in multiple projects or band managers handling several artists.
