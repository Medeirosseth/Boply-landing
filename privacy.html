Introduction

  • Group settings and preferences

Show & Event Information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Create and manage your account, enable band collaboration, store your data
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Communicate with You: Send important updates about the Service, respond to inquiries
  • Ensure Security: Protect against unauthorized access and maintain data integrity

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your financial data with advertisers

Data Storage & Security

Boply uses Google Firebase to store and process your data, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Hosting. Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Cloud Platform.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure authentication protocols, and regular security assessments.

Data Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Your Rights & Choices

Deletion: You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at contact@boply.io. We will delete your data within 30 days.
